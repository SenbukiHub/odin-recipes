Tomato Avocado Sandwich

Description

This open-faced avocado and tomato dish is made with hearty ciabatta bread and a generous sprinkling of black pepper. Dig into the juicy, boldly seasoned flavor of this wholesome toast that's too good not to share.

Prep:10 mins

Total: 10 mins

Servings: 1

Yield: 1 Sandwich

Per Serving: 335 calories; protein 8g; carbohydrates 41.5g; fat 16.9g; sodium 2156.5mg

Ingredients

  • 1/2 avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 slice ciabatta bread
  • 3 slices tomato
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix mashed avocado and garlic salt together in a bowl
  2. Spread onto ciabatta bread
  3. Layer tomatoes over avocado and top with black pepper

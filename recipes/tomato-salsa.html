Fresh Tomato Salsa

Description

A fresh tomato salsa that goes great with your favorite chips. It gets even better as the flavors meld.

Prep: 20 mins

Total: 20 mins

Serving: 40

Yield: 5 cups

Per Serving: 5 calories; protein 0.2g; carbohydrates 1.1g; sodium 25.1mg

Ingredients

  • 3 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup onion, diced
  • 1/2 chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 4 teaspoons chopped fresh jalapeno pepper (including seeds)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Stir together tomatoes, onion, green bell pepper, cilantro, lime juice, jalapeno pepper, cumin, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
